About our DBMS Project

This project is developed as part of our Database Management Systems course with the aim of building a smart, reliable, and user-friendly attendance management platform. The system is designed to automate the traditional attendance process by providing a centralized database where student participation in events and activities can be securely recorded and managed.

To develop a simple yet powerful attendance management system that streamlines event participation tracking while maintaining data integrity and ease of use for both students and administrators.

To provide an efficient digital solution that promotes accountability, encourages student engagement in events, and supports institutions in managing attendance records through modern database technologies.
